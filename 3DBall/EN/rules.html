Rules

Playing surface

The game will take place in the IUT hall.

Ball

It must be made of leather or any other suitable material.
Its circumference must be between 62 cm and 64 cm.
Its mass must not exceed 440 grams and be at least 400 grams at the start of the game.
The pressure of the ball must be in the range of 400-600 g/cm2.

Number of players

The games are played between two teams, which must be composed of 3 field players.

Game time

The game is played in 8 minutes without half-time.

Refereeing

Two referees will be present:
- the referee
- the timekeeper (assistant who manages the time, stops and restarts the clock when necessary).

Others particularities

There are no touches. The walls can be used.
In case of a tie, there will be a penalty shoot-out.
